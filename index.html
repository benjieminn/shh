About Us

Sin Hock Heng Cushion Maker was established in 1996 with the aim to provide good quality upholstery services for car enthusiast. As a business in the automotive industry, we strongly believe in transparency of our services. With our dedicated and professional team, as well a good track records of upholstering both luxury and classic cars, Sin Hock Heng Cushion Maker has built a reputable name for ourselves. Today, we provide upholstery services ranging from economic cars to high end customizable services. We have also envolved into a business that provides insurance claims for windscreen installation.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to deliver impeccable workmanship to customers every single time. We value our customers with the greatest possible care and attention to all aspects of our business.
