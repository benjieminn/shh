About Us

Founded 1984, Sin Hock Heng Cushion Maker's aim is to provide quality motor vehicle upholstery services. As a business in the automotive industry, we strongly believe in being transparent with our customers. With our dedicated and professional team, as well a good track records of upholstering both luxury and vintage cars, Sin Hock Heng Cushion Maker has built a reputable name for ourselves. With our decades of experience in upholstery service, we are able to serve customers ranging from economic cars to high end customisable workmanship. We have also envolved into a business of modifying, installating windscreen and claiming of insurance.

Warranty Coverage

At Sin Hock Heng Cushion Maker, we provide at least 6 months warranty that guarantees all products to be free of defects in material and workmanship starting on the date of purchase from us. Our warranty does not cover regular wear and tear or damages such as abrasions, scratches or fading. For any product defects besides natural wear and tear, improper use, or accidents, do drop by at our workshop, along with the original receipt. This type of repairs is free of charge.
